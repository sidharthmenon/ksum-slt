About She Loves Tech India 2019

She Loves Tech 2019 Global Startup Competition is the worldâ€™s largest startup competition focused on women and technology. With international rounds held in over 20 locations across Bangladesh, China, France, Germany, Hong Kong SAR, India, Indonesia, Israel, Latvia, Nepal, Pakistan, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, UAE, USA etc., the competition provides a platform for not only women entrepreneurs, but also startups dedicated to improving the lives of women globally.

For the first time, She Loves Tech will be stepping foot in India in association with Kerala Startup Mission, the nodal government organization for promoting the startups in the state of Kerala. KSUM will spear-head the National competition. The competition is focused on women-led or women-impact businesses. The registration for the competition has been opened and will closes by 15th June 2019.

The National Grand Challenge will be held in Integrated Startup Complex, Cochin, Kerala on 18th July 2019

Who can participate?

Startups seeking seed, angel or Series A round funding (under US$5M) with at least a minimum viable product.
(a) Any entrepreneur, male or female, who is using technology to impact women positively:
  • Are a significant proportion of end-users female?
  • Are products specifically designed with women users in mind?
  • Does the product itself address a problem that disproportionately affects women?
OR
(b) At least one female entrepreneur in founding team who is using technology to solve a problem
